                   GROUND

Legend:
  [R] = Red Projectile (YOU control with SPACE)
  === = Brown Slingshot Base
  [G] = Green Enemy (TARGET)
  |  | = Brown Pillars (destructible)
  |===| = Brown Platform (destructible)
  |   = Gray Walls (boundaries)
  === = Brown Ground (static)

Gameplay:
1. Hold SPACE to charge power
2. Release to launch red cube
3. Knock down the green enemy
4. Watch the physics destruction!

Physics Properties:
- Red Cube: Heavy (density 2.0), bouncy (restitution 0.3)
- Green Enemy: Medium weight (density 1.2), slightly bouncy
- Structure: Light wood (density 0.8-1.0), low bounce
- Ground/Walls: Static/Kinematic (immovable)
